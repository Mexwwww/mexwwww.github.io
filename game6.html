My Contributions

Moon of resurrection:

In this project, I made everything from this moon except for the model and texture.

When the player hits 0 HP, the moon will activate. After a short animation, the moon will explode, letting out a loud sound and a shockwave that kills every enemy it hits. When it's done, the player gets 1 HP back.
If not at 0 hp and you own the moon. It will follow the player and slowly spin around them, making sure that the player knows and can't forget that they own the moon.
