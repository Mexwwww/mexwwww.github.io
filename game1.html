Winter Wonder Ride

This game was made in 4 weeks with c# in unity.
with 2 developers and 4 artists

Winter Wonder Ride is a single player race game that was made in 4 weeks with c# in unity with 2 developers and 4 artists, where you can use your power up's to make it easier or be faster'. You can race against the time of other players, because all the fastest times are localy saved on the pc. The goal of the game is to make it to the end as fast as possible.

My Contributions

Vehicle Movement:

To make the vehicle movement I used: wheel colliders, a script I made to drive/steer, clamp the max rotation so it wouldn't fall over, code for the speedometer and I made it so when the vehicle is in the air, it will return its z rotation to 0, so it will always land on its wheels.

Camera movement whille following the car:

To make the Camera movement I made a script that makes the camera smoothly follow the car and get closser to the car when a wall is in the way so the camera won't go through walls

Power ups:

To make the powerups I made a script with 2 functions 1 for the speed boost and 1 for the shield power.
The speed boost works by adding vellocity in the derection the Vehicle is going when the cooldown is over and the player hits E.
The shield works by starting a coroutine that enebles the shield gameobject and slowly turning the transparency of the materail up.
After some time it slowly turns the transparency down again and once it hits 0 it also turns the gameobject off.
The player can also see how long the cooldown takes after useing a power up by looking at the bottem right of the screen.
