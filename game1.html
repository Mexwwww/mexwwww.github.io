Winter Wonder Ride

This game was made in 4 weeks with c# in unity.
with 2 developers and 4 artists

Winter Wonder Ride is a single-player racing game developed in Unity using C# over four weeks by a team of two developers and four artists. Players can use power-ups to gain an advantage by making the race easier or increasing their speed.

My Contributions

Vehicle Movement:

To implement vehicle movement, I used wheel colliders and wrote a custom script for driving and steering. I clamped the maximum rotation to prevent the vehicle from tipping over and added code for a functional speedometer. Additionally, I ensured that when the vehicle is airborne, its Z-axis rotation automatically resets to 0, allowing it to always land on its wheels

Camera movement while following the car:

I created a custom script for the camera movement, making it smoothly follow the car. The script also adjusts the camera's position by moving it closer to the car when there's a wall in the way, preventing the camera from clipping through walls.

Power ups:

To make the powerups I made a script with 2 functions: 1 for the speed boost and 1 for the shield power.
The speed boost works by adding vellocity in the derection the Vehicle is going when the cooldown is over and the player hits E.
The shield works by starting a coroutine that activates the shield GameObject and gradually increases the material's transparency.
After a set duration, the transparency slowly decreases, and once it reaches 0, the shield GameObject is deactivated.
The player can track the cooldown time after using a power-up by checking the bottom right of the screen.
